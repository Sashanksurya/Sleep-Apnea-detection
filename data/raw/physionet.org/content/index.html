Rajkumar Dhar, Shivangi Kewalramani, Larisa Tereshchenko

The software application (written in MATLAB) automatically detects pacing spikes (pacing artifacts) on a 12-lead ECG signal and automatically removes them. User-friendly GUI permits reviewing the results and adjusting settings to achieve perfection.
